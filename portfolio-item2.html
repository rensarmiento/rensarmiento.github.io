E-Commerce Website

A Large-Scale E-Commerce Replica

Click here for repository

Introduction:
The goal was to replicate a digital marketplace for users who want the latest and greatest products in terms of fashion, electronics, and more. An easy place to purchase name brand, off brand and foreign brand options. The site has a UI that is easily understandable to any user of all ages.

Project replicated a large-scale E-Commerce site with similar, working features and functionality.
Features Include:
Register and Login
Search
Review Section
Add and Remove Items to Cart
Change Quantity of Items
Purchase Items
Request Payment and Billing Information
Request and Choose Shipping Information

Problems:
Coding:
Return to home page after checking out with paypal was not linking back to the home page.
Ran into initial issues with testing
Script wasn’t working as expected
Replication of local environment
Unrealistic/Over-optimistic planning of sprints

Solutions:
During testing, few problems arose such as returning to the home page after checking out with paypal’s RESTful API.
During the building phase, each member was assigned a partner with a separate task. When coding errors were reported through the backlog during testing, the partner would assist in debugging the coding error.
If little progress was achieved, the problem would be addressed during the SCRUM meeting to raise awareness and address the issue as a whole.

Skills and Expertise Demonstrated:
Front-end Web Development (HTML, CSS, JavaScript)
Responsive design
User-centered design principles
Integration of third-party APIs
Incorporated Unit-based Testing and other Testing concepts to ensure a working project
Created an understanding of the importance of a backlog and artifacts
